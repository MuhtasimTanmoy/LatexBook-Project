\subsection{Summation and Indices}
Inline summation: $\sum_{i=0}^na_i$\\
Inline summation with displaystyle: $\displaystyle\sum_{i=0}^na_i$\\
Summation in block level math:
$$\sum_{i=0}^na_i$$
Summation in equation:
\begin{equation}
\sum_{i=0}^na_i
\end{equation}
